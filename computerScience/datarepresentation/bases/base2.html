Base 2

Base2, otherwise known as Binary, is an incredibly easy way to write numbers, because we only have two numbers to choose from. Instead of using all ten numbers from 0 - 9 like in Base10, we only use two numbers, 0 and 1 in Base2. (Hopefully you can see why Base10 is called Base10 and Base2 is called Base2 now.)

Example

510 is equal to 1012
